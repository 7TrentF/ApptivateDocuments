How to Submit a Query

Follow these steps to submit your query:

1

Navigate to Queries Tab

Open the ticket dashboard and locate the "Queries" tab.

2

Create a New Query

Click on "Create New Query" button.

3

Select Query Type

Choose between "Academic" or "Administrative" query type.

4

Select Category

Pick the most appropriate category for your query.

5

Enter Description

Write a clear description of your query. Note: You are limited to 150 characters.

6

Upload Supporting Documentation

Optional: Attach any relevant supporting documents to strengthen your query.

7

Submit

Click the "Submit" button to finalize and send your query.

Pro Tip: Be concise and clear in your description to help process your query quickly.
